**Key Insights**:
- Quality is determined at every stage — variety selection (30%), cultivation (20%), harvesting (25%), and post-harvest processing (25%). IoT sensor data helps optimize the cultivation and harvesting decisions, while the 2016 Thai processing revolution proved even Catimor can reach specialty grade through controlled fermentation. CLR is the single greatest disease threat, but IoT leaf wetness and temperature sensors enable predictive management that can reduce fungicide use by 40-60%. Specialty coffee commands 3-10x the commodity price, making selective harvesting and quality processing the most impactful investments a farmer can make
- IoT-driven irrigation using soil moisture sensors can save 30-41% water and increase yield by 15-22% compared to traditional calendar-based scheduling, with drip irrigation + IoT automation paying for itself in 4-8 months on a 10-rai Arabica farm
- Controlled fermentation with pH and temperature monitoring can add 5-10 SCA cup score points over traditional processing, unlocking specialty premiums of 150-400% above commodity prices — the fermentation tank sensor kit costs just 1,750-4,450 THB per tank
