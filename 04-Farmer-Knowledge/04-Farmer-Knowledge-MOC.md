**Key Insight**: Quality is determined at every stage — variety selection (30%), cultivation (20%), harvesting (25%), and post-harvest processing (25%). IoT sensor data helps optimize the cultivation and harvesting decisions, while the 2016 Thai processing revolution proved even Catimor can reach specialty grade through controlled fermentation. CLR is the single greatest disease threat, but IoT leaf wetness and temperature sensors enable predictive management that can reduce fungicide use by 40-60%. Specialty coffee commands 3-10x the commodity price, making selective harvesting and quality processing the most impactful investments a farmer can make.
