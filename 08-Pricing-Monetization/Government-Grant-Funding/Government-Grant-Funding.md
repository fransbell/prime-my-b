# Government & Grant Funding

> **Summary**: Complete guide to government grants, subsidies, and funding programs available for Prime My B's deployment in Northern Thailand — from DEPA OTOD community grants to BAAC loans to BOI tax incentives — with specific application strategies, timelines, and a recommended funding roadmap for Year 1-2.

---

## Overview

Government funding is the launch fuel that enables Prime My B to deploy at scale before B2B revenue reaches sustainability. Thailand's Agriculture 4.0 national strategy has created a favorable environment for smart farming technology, with multiple agencies offering grants, subsidized loans, and tax incentives specifically designed for IoT agriculture adoption. The key programs — DEPA OTOD (up to 200,000 THB per community), BAAC Community Enterprise Loan (0.01% annual interest), and BOI smart farming tax incentives — can be combined to fund a complete Prime My B deployment at near-zero cost to farmers. This document provides a strategic roadmap for accessing these programs, with specific timelines, application requirements, and a recommended sequence that maximizes funding while minimizing dependency on any single source.

## DEPA Programs — Primary Grant Source

### OTOD (One Tambon One Digital)

DEPA's OTOD program is the single most important funding source for Prime My B's launch. It provides free IoT hardware, training, and platform access to farming communities, and Prime My B can participate as a technology partner.

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Program** | OTOD (One Tambon One Digital) |
| **Agency** | DEPA (Digital Economy Promotion Agency) |
| **Seasons** | 3 completed; OTOD #3 accepting applications |
| **Grant per community** | Up to 200,000 THB |
| **What's covered** | IoT hardware, training, platform access, 6-month mentorship |
| **Eligibility** | Community groups, tambon-level applications |
| **Website** | depa.or.th |
| **Northern Thailand office** | Chiang Mai |

### Application Strategy for Prime My B

Prime My B should apply as a **technology partner** in the OTOD program, not as a grant recipient. This means:

1. **Identify coffee-growing tambons** in Chiang Mai, Chiang Rai, Mae Hong Son, and Nan that are eligible for OTOD
2. **Partner with community leaders** to submit joint applications — the community applies for the grant, and Prime My B is the designated technology provider
3. **Provide coffee-specific IoT kits** using OTOD grant funds — each 200,000 THB grant covers approximately 20-40 sensor nodes (at 5,000-10,000 THB per node)
4. **Deliver training** as part of the OTOD program's 3-5 day workshop requirement
5. **Provide 6-month mentorship** as required by the OTOD program

### OTOD Application Timeline

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Month 1 | Contact DEPA Northern Region Office (Chiang Mai) |
| 2 | Month 1-2 | Identify 2-3 coffee-growing tambons for pilot application |
| 3 | Month 2-3 | Prepare joint application with community leaders |
| 4 | Month 3-4 | Submit application; attend DEPA briefing |
| 5 | Month 4-6 | Selection and approval process |
| 6 | Month 6-7 | Deploy sensor kits; conduct training |
| 7 | Month 7-12 | Provide mentorship; collect data; validate predictions |

### DEPA d-transform & d-voucher Programs

| Program | Grant Amount | Target | Prime My B Application |
|---------|-------------|--------|----------------------|
| **Mini Digital Transformation** | 10,000 THB/firm | 400 SMEs | Register as digital tool provider; farmers use voucher for Prime My B kits |
| **d-transform** | Up to 200,000 THB/project | 600 SMEs | Apply for smart farming project funding for larger deployments |
| **d-voucher** | Subsidized amounts | 15,000 small businesses | Qualify Prime My B as a voucher-eligible digital tool |

## BAAC Loans — Near-Zero Financing

### Community Enterprise Loan

BAAC's Community Enterprise Loan at 0.01% annual interest is effectively free financing. For a coffee farmer group wanting to purchase Prime My B sensor kits, this is the ideal financial instrument.

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Interest rate** | 0.01% per year |
| **Loan amount** | No upper limit (based on group project) |
| **Eligibility** | Registered Community Enterprise (5+ members) |
| **Collateral** | Group guarantee (no individual collateral required) |
| **Processing time** | 1-2 weeks |
| **How to apply** | Any BAAC branch (Chiang Mai, Chiang Rai, Mae Hong Son, Nan) |

### Financing Example: 10-Farm Cooperative

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| 10× Coffee Health Kit (5,500 THB each) | 55,000 THB |
| 1× LoRa gateway | 3,500 THB |
| Installation and setup | 5,000 THB |
| **Total** | **63,500 THB** |
| Annual interest at 0.01% | **6.35 THB/year** |
| Monthly repayment (2-year term) | ~2,646 THB/month |
| Per-farmer monthly cost | **~265 THB/month** (or ~53 THB/month over 5 years) |

### Smart Farmer Loan

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Interest rate** | MRR (6-7%) first 5 years |
| **Maximum amount** | Up to 3,000,000 THB |
| **Term** | Up to 15 years |
| **Eligibility** | Registered farmers with Smart Farmer certification |

**Note:** The Community Enterprise Loan at 0.01% is far superior to the Smart Farmer Loan at 6-7%. Always recommend the Community Enterprise route for IoT hardware financing.

## BOI Smart Farming Incentives

### Board of Investment Benefits

The Board of Investment (BOI) offers tax and non-tax incentives for companies operating in smart farming technology. If Prime My B is structured as a technology company, it may qualify for significant tax benefits.

| Incentive | Details | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Corporate income tax exemption** | 3-8 years | Major tax savings during growth phase |
| **Import duty exemption** | On imported machinery and raw materials | Reduces sensor component costs |
| **Permission to bring in skilled workers** | Foreign experts for technology development | Access to ML/IoT specialists |
| **Permission to own land** | For technology demonstration farms | Pilot deployment facilities |
| **Permission to take out foreign currency** | International operations | Future expansion to Vietnam, Laos |

### Eligibility for Smart Farming Category

| Requirement | Prime My B Status |
|-------------|------------------|
| Minimum investment of 1,000,000 THB | ✅ Achievable with DEPA funding + initial revenue |
| Technology-driven agriculture | ✅ IoT sensors + AI predictions |
| Thai majority ownership (51%+) | Depends on company structure |
| Located in promoted zone | ✅ Northern Thailand is a promoted zone for agriculture |

## Agriculture 4.0 Integration Strategy

### Aligning with National Strategy

Agriculture 4.0 is Thailand's national framework for digital agriculture transformation. Prime My B's positioning as an Agriculture 4.0 implementation for the coffee sector creates eligibility for multiple programs and establishes credibility with government partners.

| Agriculture 4.0 Pillar | Prime My B Alignment | Funding Opportunity |
|------------------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Data Foundation** | Coffee zone sensor data feeds national database | MDES data partnership contracts |
| **Precision Farming** | Sensor-driven irrigation and fertilization | DOAE Smart Farmer program funding |
| **AI Advisory** | CLR prediction, yield/quality forecasting | NECTEC research partnership grants |
| **Market Intelligence** | Quality prediction links to pricing | DEPA digital marketplace programs |
| **Digital Finance** | Farm performance data for credit assessment | BAAC partnership contracts |

### How to Position for Government Funding

| Positioning Statement | Target Audience | Funding Result |
|----------------------|----------------|---------------|
| "Agriculture 4.0 for Thai coffee" | DEPA, MDES | OTOD grants, digital transformation funds |
| "Smart farming for highland development" | Royal Project, HRDI | Project deployment contracts |
| "IoT-verified credit assessment for coffee farmers" | BAAC | Partnership for loan assessment |
| "Data-driven CLR disease prevention" | DOA, DOAE | Research grants, extension program funding |
| "Specialty coffee quality traceability" | DIP (GI registration), exporters | GI protection funding, certification programs |

## Key Takeaways

1. **DEPA OTOD is the fastest path to funding** — 200,000 THB per community with relatively low application difficulty. Apply for 2-3 communities in Year 1.
2. **BAAC Community Enterprise Loan is the smartest financing** — 0.01% interest is effectively free. Help farmer groups register and apply.
3. **Combine programs for maximum benefit** — DEPA OTOD (hardware) + BAAC loan (expansion) + BOI (tax savings) creates a complete funding stack.
4. **Position as Agriculture 4.0 for coffee** — this framing creates eligibility across multiple agencies and aligns with national strategy.
5. **Grants are launch fuel, not business model** — target <30% of revenue from grants by Year 3. B2B revenue must become the primary source.
6. **Government partnerships create credibility** — a DEPA-funded pilot at Doi Tung or Royal Project would validate Prime My B more than any marketing.
7. **Apply early and often** — DEPA OTOD has multiple application cycles. Even rejected applications build relationships and improve future submissions.
