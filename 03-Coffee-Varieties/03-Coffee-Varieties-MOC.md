**Key Insight**: Altitude is the primary determinant — above 800m favors Arabica (Catimor, Typica), below 600m favors Robusta. The 600-800m zone is the decision boundary. Chiang Mai 80 (a Catimor-type selection backcrossed with SL28) accounts for 70-80% of Northern Thai Arabica, while Chumphon clones dominate Thai Robusta. A "Robusta Renaissance" is underway with GI-registered Robusta seeing 7.5x price increases.
