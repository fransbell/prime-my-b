# Brand Identity — Style Guide

---

## Primary Logo

The primary logo for **Harvest Lanna Plus** is the cornerstone of the brand identity. It features a coffee bean shape with a mountain peak rendered inside, using a two-tone color scheme of dark brown and muted gold. A **"+"** symbol in gold is positioned in the upper right of the bean icon, signifying improvement and growth.

- **Icon**: Coffee bean with mountain peak silhouette
- **Accent**: "+" symbol in Muted Gold
- **Main Wordmark**: "HARVEST" — Dark green, bold, uppercase (Montserrat Bold)
- **Sub Wordmark**: "LANNA PLUS" — Muted Gold, uppercase (Montserrat SemiBold)
- **Tagline**: "COFFEE QUALITY IMPROVEMENT" — Uppercase, flanked by horizontal rules

### Primary Logo Usage Guidelines

- Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo (minimum 1x the height of the icon)
- Do not stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
- Do not apply drop shadows, gradients, or other effects not specified in this guide
- The logo should always appear on a clean background with sufficient contrast

## Typography

### Heading Font — Montserrat

| Property       | Value                          |
|----------------|--------------------------------|
| Font Family    | Montserrat                     |
| Weights Used   | Bold, SemiBold                 |
| Characteristics| Clean, Modern, Confident       |
| Primary Color  | Forest Green (#1E3D2F)         |

**Usage**: Headlines, subheadings, wordmarks, navigation labels, and any text that requires emphasis or hierarchy. Montserrat's geometric structure and bold presence make it ideal for communicating authority and modernity while remaining approachable.

### Body Font — Lato

| Property       | Value                          |
|----------------|--------------------------------|
| Font Family    | Lato                           |
| Weights Used   | Regular, Light                 |
| Characteristics| Readable, Friendly, Professional|
| Primary Color  | Dark Gray                      |

**Usage**: Body copy, paragraphs, descriptions, captions, and any extended reading text. Lato's humanist-inspired design offers warmth and legibility, ensuring that longer passages of text remain comfortable to read across both print and digital media.

### Type Hierarchy

1. **H1 / Display** — Montserrat Bold, Forest Green, uppercase
2. **H2 / Section Headings** — Montserrat SemiBold, Forest Green
3. **Body Text** — Lato Regular, Dark Gray
4. **Captions / Labels** — Lato Light, Dark Gray
5. **Taglines** — Montserrat SemiBold, uppercase, letter-spacing increased

## Color Palette

The Harvest Lanna Plus color palette draws from the natural world of coffee cultivation — rich soil, verdant highlands, and warm harvest tones. Every color has been selected to evoke quality, sustainability, and craftsmanship.

### Forest Green

| Format | Value                          |
|--------|--------------------------------|
| HEX    | `#1E3D2F`                      |
| CMYK   | C 85 · M 50 · Y 78 · K 56     |

Forest Green is the primary brand color. It communicates growth, stability, and a deep connection to the land. Use it for headings, key UI elements, primary buttons, and the "HARVEST" wordmark. This color should dominate any brand composition and serve as the visual anchor.

### Espresso Brown

| Format | Value                          |
|--------|--------------------------------|
| HEX    | `#3B2A1E`                      |
| CMYK   | C 52 · M 67 · Y 78 · K 71     |

Espresso Brown is the secondary brand color. It evokes the richness of roasted coffee and fertile earth. Use it for the coffee bean icon, supporting text, borders, and subtle accents. It pairs naturally with Forest Green and Muted Gold to create a grounded, earthy palette.

### Muted Gold

| Format | Value                          |
|--------|--------------------------------|
| HEX    | `#CBA65A`                      |
| CMYK   | C 17 · M 28 · Y 74 · K 0      |

Muted Gold is the accent color. It represents quality, achievement, and the warmth of a golden harvest. Use it for the "LANNA PLUS" wordmark, the "+" symbol, highlights, CTAs, and any element that needs to draw attention without overpowering. It provides a refined contrast against both Forest Green and Espresso Brown.

### Warm Ivory

| Format | Value                          |
|--------|--------------------------------|
| HEX    | `#F5F3ED`                      |
| CMYK   | C 4 · M 4 · Y 7 · K 0         |

Warm Ivory is the background color. It provides a soft, natural canvas that reduces visual fatigue and allows the brand colors to stand out. Use it for page backgrounds, card surfaces, and any large areas of negative space. It ensures the design feels approachable and organic rather than sterile.

### Color Combinations

| Pairing                    | Use Case                                      |
|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Forest Green on Warm Ivory | Primary headings, navigation                  |
| Espresso Brown on Warm Ivory | Body text, icons, subtle accents            |
| Muted Gold on Forest Green  | Highlighted text, badges, featured elements |
| Muted Gold on Espresso Brown| Premium labels, certification marks          |
| Forest Green on Muted Gold  | Bold statements, seasonal campaigns          |

## Iconography

Icons follow a simple, clean line-art style with minimal detail. They are designed to be easily recognizable at small sizes and to maintain consistency with the brand's earthy, organic aesthetic. Each icon should be rendered in Espresso Brown or Forest Green, depending on the context.

### Sorting

- **Visual**: Coffee beans in a circular container with a checkmark
- **Meaning**: Quality through selection
- **Usage**: Quality control sections, product sorting descriptions, certification badges

### Roasting

- **Visual**: Coffee roaster with steam lines rising
- **Meaning**: Precision for optimal profile
- **Usage**: Process descriptions, roasting details, craft and precision messaging

### Growth

- **Visual**: Plant sprouting from soil
- **Meaning**: Sustainable improvement from farm to future
- **Usage**: Sustainability sections, growth metrics, environmental messaging, future vision

### Icon Design Principles

1. **Simplicity** — Use the minimum number of strokes needed to convey meaning
2. **Consistency** — Maintain uniform stroke width (2px at 24x24 viewbox) across all icons
3. **Alignment** — Ensure optical alignment within the icon grid
4. **Color** — Default to Espresso Brown; use Muted Gold for active/highlighted states
5. **Spacing** — Maintain consistent padding within the icon bounding box

## General Brand Guidelines

### Tone of Voice

The Harvest Lanna Plus brand voice is confident yet approachable, knowledgeable yet humble. All communications should reflect the values of quality, partnership, and sustainability. Avoid jargon that alienates; prefer language that empowers and educates.

### Photography Style

- Natural lighting with warm tones
- Authentic, unposed shots of coffee farms, processing, and communities
- Close-up textures of coffee beans, soil, and leaves
- Avoid overly stylized or artificial imagery

### Spacing & Layout

- Use generous white space to create a clean, uncluttered appearance
- Align elements to a consistent grid system
- Maintain visual hierarchy through size, weight, and color contrast
- The Warm Ivory background should always dominate the composition

### Do's and Don'ts

| Do                              | Don't                             |
|---------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Use the logo as provided        | Re-create or modify the logo      |
| Maintain clear space around logo| Crowd the logo with other elements|
| Use brand colors as specified    | Introduce unapproved colors       |
| Use Montserrat for headings     | Mix decorative fonts              |
| Keep layouts clean and spacious | Overcrowd the design              |
| Use Warm Ivory as primary background | Use pure white (#FFFFFF) as background |
